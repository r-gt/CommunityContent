EASE STUDIO!v1.0

Ease studio a front-end tool to make concatenated animation paths using easelib on Vircon32.

Properties and exporting can be found in the menu.

CONTROLS:

A key: add keyframe.



Right click: remove keyframe.



Drag: move timeline.



shift + scroll: zoom in / zoom out.



scroll: move time indicator.



Click on top: move time indicator.



space: play / pause.
